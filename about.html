What does PlantRealm offer?

At PlantRealm we offer a variety of well crafted selection of indoor plants for your terrariums as well as for your living space.

We provide you with quality products and supplies with the opportunity to learn and build with us or at home.

PlantRealm adds a refreshing flavour to the world of terrariums. We introduce and offer a variety of themed miniatures. Create your own realm with our fun selection of miniatures.
